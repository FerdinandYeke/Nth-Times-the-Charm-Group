About Recipeté

Recipeté is a grocery delivery app designed to make meal preparation simple and stress-free. Whether you're a busy professional, a parent on the go, or someone who struggles with planning and shopping, Recipeté helps you cook delicious meals with ease.


Our Vision: Our product is a grocery delivery app that allows you to easily obtain ingredients for some of your favorite recipes. Recipeté is built for anyone looking to simplify meal prep. Are you balancing a busy schedule, struggling to plan meals, or simply wanting a faster way to get fresh ingredients right to your door? Use our recipes or upload your own!

Key Features & Benefits

Select Ingredients

Pick what you have on hand, and we’ll generate tasty recipes instantly.

Upload Recipes

Found something delicious online? Have a secret family recipe? Upload it and order the ingredients in minutes.

Fast Delivery

Fresh ingredients delivered right to your door, ready for cooking.

Breath Easy

Need support with executive functioning? We take care of planning, organization, help with initiation, and time management.
