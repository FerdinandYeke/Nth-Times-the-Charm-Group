Personas

Persona 1: Jess

Retired woman • Volunteers part time • Only cooks for two people • Not tech-saavy

Jess Dove is an elderly woman in her late 60's. Jess is retired, where she does not work. Jess also only cooks for two people, where meal size and cooking time is not by much. Jess's profession before retirement was Professional Chef. Graduating from Sam Houston State University in 1970 from its culinary program at age 23. Jess then worked at a local restaurant in Houston then worked at multi-star restaurant in Houston and at New York City for about 40 years. During this time, Jess married in 1978 to Marc Vas at age 31 in Austin, Texas. Marc Vas is an architect who graduated from Rice University with a master's in architecture and design in 1970.

During her time as a chef, Jess had little experience with handing stuff digitally based on the restaurants that she had worked at. Though she believes that things digitally handled with restaurants can be beneficial, she prefers doing things more traditionally, like interacting with customers, taking orders, knowing somewhat about the customer and cooking the dish, while also being familiar with the restaurant atmosphere.

Persona 2: Rachel

Parent with young kids • Does not work • Deals with depression • Not tech-saavy

Rachel, age 40, is a stay-at-home mom with two children; Tina age 8 and Dale age 3. She was born in Hampton, New Hampshire, to middle class parents. She grew up having sit down dinners every night and feels guilty that she doesn’t provide that for her children. Her husband, Ben, is a software engineer who commutes to work by train. This typically takes an hour one way. Rachel is the primary grocery shopper and cooks most meals for her household. She must balance managing a household, driving kids to and from school, picking her husband up at the train station, and family social activities.

Rachel was recently diagnosed with depression, which causes her to struggle with executive dysfunction. She uses social media but is uncomfortable with technology. She would not describe herself as a “computer person”. She frequently gets recipes by googling “easy meals”, “weeknight meals”, and “dinners for picky eaters”. She has some family recipes that have been passed down to her that are frequent favorites in her household. She tries to not spend too much on groceries but would be willing to pay a little extra to decrease her mental load.

Persona 3: Isaac

Single man in his early 30s • Works full-time • Semi-tech saavy

Isaac, age 32, is an electrical engineer from Utica, New York. He earned his bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering technology from SUNY Polytechnic Institute in 2016 and has been working since. He cooks for himself, as he believes it’s healthier than takeout. He is not married.

Issac often can’t find the time to go to the store and buy his ingredients due to how often he works and has to settle for takeout instead. He has experience with technology, but has never used an online delivery app before. When he does cook, he uses recipes his coworkers tell him about as well as his own. Isacc takes pride in his cooking and calls his meals “a convergence of flavor”.

Persona 4: Tyler

College student • Full-time student • Has ADHD • Tech-savvy

Tyler, 19 years old, goes to Colorado University in Boulder, CO. He is currently getting a degree in Computer Science with Minors in Physics and Mathematics. Tyler has been attending for 3 semesters and is a sophomore and isn’t too great at cooking but is super tech savvy and knows his way around electronics.

Busy with assignments, projects, and internship hunting, Tyler doesn’t have a whole lot of time to handle cooking and meal prepping but wants to get better with that. He’s very comfortable using apps and technology and is very good at math but gets distracted very quickly making it difficult for him to get much done including cooking.
